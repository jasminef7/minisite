About Me

Hi! I'm Jasmine and a student here at the University of California, Los Angeles. I am currently in my fourth year majoring in English Literature and minoring in Digital Humanities. Expecting to graduate shortly in the spring, I hope to explore career options in marketing–seeing that I am the social media manager for a student-run magazine called Pacific Ties and I've had the wonderful opportunity to intern with Digital Media Management here in Los Angeles where I was able to work on the social media for season 4 of The Kardasians, season 3 of The D'amelio Show, and Tell Me Lies on Hulu.
