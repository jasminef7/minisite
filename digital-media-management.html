Digital Media Management

Jasmine Fung

During fall quarter of 2023, I was a social media intern for an LA-based digital agency where I was able to work on the social media campaigns for season 4 of The Kardashians, season 3 of The D'amelio Show, and helped out with a show called Tell Me Lies on Hulu. I wrote copy for social media assets, operated community management on all relevant social media platforms, and dabbled with content creation.
